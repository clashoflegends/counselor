# Privacy Policy - Counselor (Clash of Legends desktop client)

_Last updated: 2026-07-23_

Counselor is the desktop client for the play-by-mail strategy game *Clash of Legends*. It communicates
**only** with the game's own server at `clashlegends.com`. It uses **no** third-party analytics,
advertising, or tracking, and it does not sell or share your data with anyone.

## What Counselor sends, and why

When you **submit your orders** to the game server, the upload includes:
- your **turn file and the orders you entered** (the purpose of the app);
- your **player login and per-player upload token** (to authenticate the submission as yours);
- the **game and turn identifiers** the orders belong to;
- basic **client and environment details** used only to support and improve the app: Counselor and
  library versions, Java version, operating-system version, screen size/scaling, install type, UI
  theme and map settings, app startup time, and how the turn file was loaded.

When Counselor **crashes**, it may send a **crash report** (the error and stack trace, plus your player
login and the current game when known) to help diagnose and fix the problem.

When you enable character portraits, Counselor **downloads** the portrait pack from the game server, and
it checks the game's GitHub Releases page for a newer version (downloaded only when you choose to update).

## What Counselor does NOT do
- No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking, ever.
- No background or automatic data collection beyond the actions above (submitting orders, reporting a
  crash, or an update/portrait check you initiate).
- No access to files other than the game files you open and the app's own settings.

## Who holds the data
The data is received and held by the *Clash of Legends* game operators (the "GM Team") solely to run the
game and support players. For questions, contact the GM Team via `clashlegends.com`.
